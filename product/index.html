Subscription Engine

Powerful Billing Logic & Automation

Our subscription engine is designed to handle the most complex billing scenarios with ease. Support multiple billing models including flat-rate, tiered, volume-based, and usage-based pricing. Create custom billing cycles that match your business model—monthly, quarterly, annual, or completely custom periods. The engine automatically calculates prorated charges for plan changes, mid-cycle upgrades, downgrades, and cancellations with precision.

Automate invoice generation, delivery, and payment collection. Configure custom tax rules, discounts, coupons, and promotional offers with granular control. The system handles complex scenarios such as add-on billing, trial periods, and grandfathered pricing automatically. Built-in dunning management with intelligent retry schedules maximizes payment recovery rates while maintaining positive customer relationships.

Revenue Management

Complete Revenue Visibility & Control

Track and manage every dollar of revenue with our comprehensive revenue management system. Automatically recognize revenue in accordance with ASC 606 and IFRS 15 accounting standards. Generate detailed financial reports that provide complete visibility into deferred revenue, recognized revenue, and future revenue projections.

Seamlessly integrate with popular accounting platforms including QuickBooks, Xero, and NetSuite for automated financial reconciliation. Export transaction data in various formats for compliance and audit purposes. Support multi-entity revenue consolidation for organizations with multiple business units or subsidiaries. Built-in audit trails maintain complete records of all revenue-related transactions for compliance and internal controls.

Customer Lifecycle

End-to-End Customer Journey Management

Manage the entire customer lifecycle from initial signup through renewal, expansion, and eventual churn. Provide customers with self-service portals where they can view invoices, update payment methods, change plans, and manage subscriptions independently—reducing support overhead and improving customer satisfaction.

Implement automated retention workflows that identify at-risk customers and trigger targeted engagement campaigns. Track customer health scores based on usage patterns, payment history, and engagement metrics. Enable seamless upgrade and downgrade experiences that encourage expansion while accommodating customer needs. Comprehensive customer profiles provide complete visibility into subscription history, interactions, and preferences.
